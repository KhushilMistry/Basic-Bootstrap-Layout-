ABOUT SYNAPSE

Dhirubhai Ambani Institute of Information and Communication Technology (DA-IICT), primarily known for research and development, has as the torch bearer among the premier technological institutions of Gujarat over the past 14 years. In these years of enrolling and training some of the best students of the country, DA-IICT owes its name and fame to its students, as well as to its dedicated and excellent faculty. Our students have brought and continue to bring to our residential campus at Gandhinagar their immense youthful energy.
